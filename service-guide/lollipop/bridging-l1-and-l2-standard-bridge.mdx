---
title: Bridging L1 and L2 (Standard Bridge)
---

Lollipop supports Standard Bridge for moving assets between Ethereum L1 and Lollipop L2. Users can send ETH and ERC20 tokens to L2 via a Standard Bridge contract (deposit). Conversely, users can transfer ETH, ERC20 tokens from L2 to L1 via Standard Bridge (withdraw).
